We're Officially Open in Armidale!

This one means a lot to us.

When we started Asina, we had a simple belief — that every person deserves support that actually fits their life, not the other way around. Support that's built around who you are, what you enjoy, and where you want to go.

Today, we're so proud to say that Asina is officially open and accepting participants in Armidale and the New England region.

Armidale is a community we've always admired — tight-knit, welcoming, and full of heart. It feels like the perfect place to grow, and we can't wait to become part of the fabric here.

So, what can we help with? Our services in Armidale include:

Supported Independent Living (SIL) — helping you live your way in your own home
Community Access & Participation — getting out and about doing the things you love
Daily Living Support — a hand with the everyday things that matter
Meal Preparation — nutritious meals tailored to your tastes and needs
Transport — getting you where you need to go, when you need to be there
Capacity Building — growing your skills and confidence for the future
Drop-In Support — flexible support when you need it

Whether you're a participant looking for a provider who truly listens, a family wanting peace of mind, or a support coordinator searching for a team that goes above and beyond — we'd love to have a chat.

This is just the beginning for Asina in Armidale, and we're excited to walk this journey alongside you.
