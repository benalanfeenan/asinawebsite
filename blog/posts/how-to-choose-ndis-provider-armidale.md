## Choosing the Right Provider Matters

Your NDIS provider has a huge impact on your quality of life. The right provider will help you reach your goals, respect your choices, and deliver support that actually fits your routine. The wrong one can leave you feeling unheard and stuck.

In Armidale, there are several NDIS providers to choose from. Here's how to figure out which one is the best fit for you.

## Questions Worth Asking

Before signing up with any provider, have a conversation with them. A good provider will welcome questions and be upfront with their answers.

**How long have you been operating?** New providers aren't necessarily bad, but experience matters. Ask about their team's background in disability support.

**What's your staff turnover like?** High turnover means you'll constantly be meeting new support workers. Consistency is important for building trust and routine.

**Can I meet my support worker first?** A good provider will let you meet potential workers before they start. Chemistry matters.

**How do you handle complaints?** Every provider should have a clear complaints process. If they can't explain it simply, that's a red flag.

**Do you have a participant portal?** Being able to see your shift notes, invoices, and budget in real time gives you control. Not all providers offer this.

**What's your approach to goal setting?** Your support should be tied to your NDIS goals. Ask how they track progress and adjust support over time.

## Red Flags to Watch For

- They pressure you to sign up quickly without explaining your options.
- They can't give you a straight answer about pricing or what's included.
- They don't involve you in choosing your support workers.
- They have no online presence or reviews.
- They don't offer flexibility in scheduling.

## What Sets a Good Provider Apart
