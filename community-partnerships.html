Community Partnerships

Stronger Together: Our Local Partners

We believe great disability support doesn't happen in isolation. We're building real partnerships with Armidale businesses, organisations, and community groups to create opportunities for our participants.

Local First Community Driven Armidale & Beyond NDIS Funded
Why We Partner

Building an Ecosystem of Support

Asina actively seeks partnerships with local businesses to create volunteering opportunities, work experience placements, social outings, and community connections for participants. This isn't just about branding — it's about building a genuine ecosystem of support in the New England Region.

We know that real change happens when organisations work together. When a local cafe hires our participant for work experience, when a community group welcomes them to activities, when a business gives them the chance to contribute — that's when lives transform.

Benefits of Partnering

What You Get From Us

  • Support disability inclusion in your business and community
  • Access enthusiastic volunteers & workers who bring genuine passion to their roles
  • Join a growing community network of like-minded organisations
  • Featured on our website & social media as valued partners
  • Co-marketing opportunities to reach community-minded audiences
  • Peace of mind knowing you're supporting quality disability services

Impact & Outcomes

Partnership Benefits

For Participants

  • Access to real-world experiences in local community
  • Meaningful social connections & friendships
  • Employment & volunteering pathways
  • Increased confidence & independence
  • Sense of belonging & contribution

For Businesses

  • Support disability inclusion in your workplace
