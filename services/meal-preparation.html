Meal Preparation

Personalised meal planning, cooking skills, and dietary support — because good nutrition is the foundation of a good life.

Police Checked WWCC Cleared Food Safety NDIS Screened

What's Included

Food, Skills & Nutrition

Our Meal Preparation program helps you eat well, learn cooking skills, and understand nutrition for better health and independence.

Meal Planning

Creating balanced weekly meal plans tailored to your preferences and dietary needs.

Cooking Together

Hands-on cooking lessons building skills and confidence in the kitchen.

Dietary Requirements

Support for allergies, intolerances, cultural preferences and special diets.

Grocery Shopping

Assistance with shopping trips and learning to choose nutritious foods on budget.

Kitchen Skills

Building practical kitchen skills like food safety, preparation and equipment use.

Nutrition Education

Learning about balanced diets, food groups, and making healthy choices.

Special Occasions

Preparing special meals for celebrations and enjoying cooking for family and friends.

How It Works

Four Simple Steps

1

Free Consultation

We meet with you, your family, or support coordinator to understand your goals and dietary needs.

2

Personalised Plan

We create a tailored meal plan and cooking support plan aligned with your NDIS goals.

3

Cooking Sessions

We start hands-on cooking lessons and meal preparation support with consistency and care.

4

Ongoing Growth

Continue building cooking confidence and independence with regular reviews and new recipes.

Eligibility & NDIS Funding

  • You are an NDIS participant with meal preparation funding in your plan
  • Core Supports: Assistance with Daily Life Activities
  • Capacity Building: Improved Daily Life Skills & Functioning
  • Capacity Building: Increased Social & Community Participation
  • Not sure about your funding? We can help you understand your plan and categories
