What's Included

Everything You Need for Building

Our Capacity Building program is designed to help you build confidence, develop new skills, and achieve your NDIS goals.

Life Skills Training

Build practical skills for daily living and independence.

Social Skills

Develop confidence in social situations and meaningful connections.

Employment Readiness

Prepare for work experience and employment opportunities.

Money Management

Learn budgeting, saving, and financial independence.

Public Transport Training

Gain confidence and independence using public transport.

Decision Making

Build confidence in making your own choices.

Self-Advocacy

Learn to communicate your needs and speak up for yourself.

Technology Skills

Master useful technology for modern daily living.

How It Works

Four Simple Steps

1

Free Consultation

We meet with you, your family, or support coordinator to understand your goals and what matters most.

2

Personalised Plan

We create a tailored support plan that aligns with your NDIS goals and the things you love doing.

3

Worker Matching

You're paired with a support worker who gets you — someone who shares your interests and respects your pace.

4

Get Started

Begin receiving quality support with consistent, caring workers and regular progress check-ins.

Eligibility & NDIS Funding

  • You are an NDIS participant with capacity building funding in your plan
  • Capacity Building: Increased Life Skills
  • Capacity Building: Increased Social and Community Participation
  • Core Supports: Assistance with Life Skills Development
  • Not sure about your funding? We can help you understand your plan and categories
