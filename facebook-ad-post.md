**POST COPY:**

People sometimes ask me why I started Asina.

The honest answer is one family.

Early in my career, I supported a young man moving into his very first home. He'd lived with his aging parents his whole life, and they'd made this incredibly brave decision to — in their words — "let their son go."

They were terrified. Terrified his routine would fall apart. Terrified he wouldn't be cared for properly. Terrified they were making a mistake.

But we built a rapport, him and me. A really strong one. We went grocery shopping together — something his parents never thought he'd do. We cooked dinner side by side. We went on walks around the neighbourhood. Slowly, bit by bit, his world started opening up in ways nobody expected.

Then one day, his parents pulled me aside and thanked me for caring for their little boy.

I cried. I'm not too proud to say that.

That moment has stayed with me every single day since. It's the reason I built Asina Disability Support — because I wanted to make sure that kind of care wasn't just something one person could give. I wanted to build a team where it's the standard.

Every member of our team is Police Checked, WWCC cleared, First Aid & CPR certified, and NDIS Worker Screened. But more than that — they genuinely care. That's not negotiable here.

If you or someone you love is looking for disability support in Armidale, Newcastle, Tamworth, or the New England region — I'd love to have a conversation. No pressure, no sales pitch. Just a chat about what good support could look like for your family.
