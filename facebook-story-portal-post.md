**POST COPY:**

Hi, I'm Ben Feenan — founder of Asina Disability Support.

After spending 10 years working on the frontline in disability support, I started Asina because of one family, one participant, and my very first SIL home.

His parents thanked me for caring for their little boy. They knew they wouldn't be around forever, but they felt safe and secure knowing he was in a safe place. That moment changed everything for me — and it's the reason Asina exists today.

I built Asina around three values: Innovate. Create. Grow. Every decision we make comes back to giving participants and their families the kind of support I'd want for my own.

We currently have capacity across our services, including:

- Supported Independent Living (SIL)
- Community Access & Social Participation
- Assistive Technology & Equipment
- Daily Living Support & In-Home Care
- Transport Assistance
- Meal Preparation
- Capacity Building

And because I know firsthand how important it is for families to stay connected, we've built Family Portals into Asina from day one. Your portal gives you real-time access to schedules, shift notes, NDIS budget tracking, invoices, and updates — all in one place. Complete transparency, zero guesswork.

We're taking on new participants now across Armidale, Newcastle, Tamworth, and the wider New England region. If you or someone you love is looking for disability support — or you just want to have a chat about what's possible — I'd genuinely love to hear from you. No pressure, no sales pitch.
