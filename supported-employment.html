About Asina Industries

What Is Asina Industries?

Asina Industries is the business services arm of Asina Disability Support. We run real businesses — a professional car wash & detailing service and an end of lease cleaning team — that give NDIS participants the chance to build real skills, earn real income, and develop lasting confidence.

Unlike traditional "work experience" programs, these are real businesses delivering real services to real customers in the Armidale community. Participants aren't practising — they're working. Each service stream comes with wrap-around support, structured training, and pathways to open employment.

This isn't a simulation. These are real businesses, staffed by real people, serving real customers. Asina Industries exists because everyone deserves the dignity and purpose that comes with meaningful work.

The Journey

How It Works

Four simple steps from interest to employment

1

Express Interest

Fill out the form or call us. We'll chat about your goals and whether this is a good fit for you.

2

Assessment & Planning

We work with you, your support coordinator, and family to build a personalised plan aligned with your NDIS funding.

3

Training & Onboarding

You'll receive hands-on training in your chosen industry stream — from safety and tools to customer service and quality standards.

4

Begin your Asina Industries journey with ongoing support, regular check-ins, and a clear pathway to greater independence.

NDIS Funding & Eligibility

Capacity Building: Employment Support

This program falls under the "Finding and Keeping a Job" category of NDIS Capacity Building funding. It's designed to help you develop skills and confidence for sustainable employment.

Increased Social and Community Participation

Beyond employment skills, the program also supports goals around community inclusion, social connections, and independence.
